Tech4Good wins a $5,000 grant from the Princeton Class of 1978 Foundation to bring the program to Accra, Ghana

We're thrilled to share that Tech4Good has been awarded a $5,000 grant from the Princeton Class of 1978 Foundation to launch our summer program on the ground in Accra, Ghana.

This grant makes it possible to turn an idea into a real, tuition-free program, covering the cost of workspace, equipment, and mentorship as we train ambitious young technologists to build technology that serves their own communities. From day one, our students will work on real problems alongside experienced mentors, culminating in a demo day where they showcase what they've built.

To the Princeton Class of 1978 Foundation: thank you for believing in this vision and for investing in the talent that is everywhere in Ghana. This is only the beginning, and we can't wait to show you what our students create this summer.
