Turning technical talent into ethical livelihoods.

An initiative equipping young people in Ghana with the skills, mentorship, and pathways to build ethical careers in technology.

41%
The Problem
of Ghana's 41,000+ cybercrime cases between 2019 and 2023 were online fraud — disproportionately committed by young people with technical talent and no ethical pathway.
The Why

Same skills. Different futures.

Many young Ghanaians with real technical ability are never shown what an honest, ambitious career in technology looks like. Tech4Good is here to change that by providing mentorship and guidance towards a successful career path in tech.
