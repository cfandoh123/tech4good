Partner with Us

Build the future of Ghanaian technology alongside us

Tech4Good pairs ambitious young technologists in Accra with real problems worth solving. Our partners provide the mentorship, tools, internships, and funding that turn a summer of learning into lasting impact.

Why partner

A partnership that compounds

When you invest in Tech4Good, you're not funding a one-off program — you're growing a pipeline of ethical, capable technologists who go on to build for their own communities, and who remember who backed them first.

01

Sponsor a cohort

Fund seats, laptops, workspace, and stipends for a group of students. Your support directly covers the cost of running a full summer program.

02

Offer internships

Give our top graduates their first real-world engineering or product role. We help you find, vet, and onboard talent that's ready to contribute.

03

Share expertise

Run a workshop, judge a demo day, or lend engineers as mentors. A few hours from your team can reshape a student's entire trajectory.

04

Provide tools & credits

Cloud credits, software licenses, API access, and hardware let our students build ambitiously without cost getting in the way.

05

Host a demo day

Open your space for our end-of-program showcase, where students present the technology they've built to the wider community.

06

Co-design a challenge

Bring a real problem from your organization and let a student team tackle it — you get fresh ideas, they get authentic experience.
